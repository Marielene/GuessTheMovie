Hey There! Welcome to Flixle.io.
Its a really simple game.
Watch the heavily pixelated film clip and guess what the film the clip is from. If you are struggling you can hit the 'DE-PIXELATE' button and clear up the image. Each time you depixelate, your rank will drop. So keep your eyes peeled for that A+ rating.
